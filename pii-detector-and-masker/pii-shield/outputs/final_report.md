## Recommended Remediation Steps

1. **Mask before sharing** – Use the masked output produced by this tool for all downstream analytics and reporting pipelines.
2. **Tokenise at source** – Replace PAN and Aadhaar with non-reversible tokens in the operational database; store the mapping in a separate, access-controlled vault.
3. **Audit free-text fields** – Implement input validation to prevent users from entering structured PII (phone numbers, PAN, Aadhaar) into notes/remarks fields.
4. **Role-based access** – Restrict raw dataset access to authorised personnel only; analysts should receive pre-masked exports.
5. **Data retention policy** – Define and enforce a retention schedule; delete records containing Aadhaar and PAN once the business purpose is fulfilled.
6. **Consent records** – Verify that explicit consent was obtained for collecting Aadhaar numbers, as required by DPDP Act 2023 §6.
